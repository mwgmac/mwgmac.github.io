Hi, I'm Matthew! My pronouns are he/him or they/them.

One thing you should know: I am not on your side. When the opossums put us primates in our rightful place, I shall welcome our didelphine overlords with exuberant joy. I also like other cute animals (raccoons, cats, dogs, rats, &c.).

## fun facts
* I originally grew my moustache as an inside joke.
* I am a convert to Anglicanism (from atheism). David is my baptismal name.
* If you meet me, you'll notice I have strabismus. Specifically, I have alternating esotropia.
* I'm a bartender, but I hate being drunk.
* I used to write music as a hobby, and I keep meaning to get back into it.

Favourites:
* Beer: rich (malty, smoky) cask ale, yeast-forward ale, sour beer
* Time of day: about quarter to five in the morning, in summer.
* Cigarettes: Gaulois blondes
	
## my name
My forenames are Matthew William George David. In contexts where you'd normally give your full name, my preference is for 'Matthew W. G. D. McClure'. If you're into the whole brevity thing, I prefer the nonstandard 'Hew' to 'Matt' or 'Matty'. My surname is McClure, pronounced to rhyme with 'moor' (irrespective of whether you have the 'poor'/'pour' merger, in case you're a nerd), which is romanised from Gaelic—there are a number of forms. My favourite variant is 'MacLure'.
