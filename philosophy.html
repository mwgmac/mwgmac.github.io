I study MA (Hons) Philosophy at the University of Edinburgh. For the most part, I'm interested in what's often called 'analytic' philosophy. I'm an undergraduate, and know relatively little about the field, but I like the idea that philosophy is more an activity than a body of knowledge (as Wittgenstein puts it).

As well as studying philosophy, I am involved in a couple of philosophical organisations:

  • I'm Treasurer and Proceedings Organiser at the Edinburgh University Philosophy Society
  • I run the Edinburgh logic group
  • I'm pretty heavily involved in the Scotland and North East England Philosophy Association
  • I'm Typesetting Editor for the British Undergraduate Philosophy Review
