My name

My forenames are Matthew William George. In contexts where you'd normally give your full name, my preference is for 'Matthew W. G. McClure'. If you're into the whole brevity thing, I prefer the nonstandard 'Hew' to 'Matt' or 'Matty'. My surname is McClure (to rhyme with 'moor'), which is romanised from Gaelic (there are a number of forms). My favourite variant is 'MacLure'.
